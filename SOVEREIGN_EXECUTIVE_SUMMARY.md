## What Is SOVEREIGN

SOVEREIGN is a three-year autonomous security program
for Haven-1, Haven-2, and the Artificial Gravity Station.
It finds vulnerabilities before adversaries do, simulates
nation-state attacks against a digital twin, and autonomously
patches the vulnerabilities it finds — in orbit, without
rebooting, without crew disruption.

## Why This Matters for Vast

Haven-1 launches with four crew.
Haven-2 carries eight.
The Artificial Gravity Station carries forty permanently.

The cost of a security failure scales with every person
added to the mission. SOVEREIGN's cost does not.

A breach on Haven-1 costs an estimated $2B+.
SOVEREIGN costs $532K in year one.
That is a 3,700x return on the first incident alone.

More importantly: four crew members cannot wait
eight minutes for ground to respond to an attack.
SOVEREIGN responds in ninety seconds.
Autonomously. Without a reboot.
That capability does not exist anywhere else.
